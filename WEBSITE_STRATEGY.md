### Service Page Template

Each service page (`/services/[service-name]/`) includes:

1. **Hero Section**
   - Service-specific headline
   - Compelling subheading
   - Clear CTA button
   - Background imagery

2. **Service Overview**
   - What's included
   - Why clients choose this
   - Key benefits (3-4 main points)

3. **Detailed Process**
   - Step-by-step breakdown
   - Timeline expectations
   - What to expect during project
   - Prep requirements

4. **Why Choose Delgados** (Service-Specific)
   - Expertise in this area
   - Materials/techniques used
   - Guarantees/warranty for this service
   - Local Orange County success stories

5. **Benefits Section**
   - Visual + text benefits
   - Long-term value
   - Problem-solution pairs

6. **Project Gallery**
   - Before/after images (service-specific)
   - Location tags (Orange County areas)
   - Brief descriptions

7. **FAQs (Service-Specific)**
   - Common questions for THIS service
   - Cost ranges
   - Timeline
   - Maintenance tips

8. **Trust/Credibility Section**
   - Certifications for this service
   - Insurance coverage details
   - Warranty for this service
   - Testimonials (specific to service)

9. **Call to Action**
   - Schedule consultation
   - Free estimate button
   - Phone number
   - Contact form

## Design System - Premium Minimalist Aesthetic

### Color Palette
- **Primary Navy**: #0A192F (Deep, trustworthy)
- **Accent Gold**: #F59E0B (Premium, warm)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF, #F9FAFB, #F3F4F6 (Clean, minimal)
- **Text**: #0A192F (Primary), #4B5563 (Secondary), #9CA3AF (Tertiary)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Space Grotesk (bold, modern, premium)
- **Body**: Inter (clean, readable, professional)
- **Sizes**: Generous hierarchy, careful spacing

### Spacing & Layout
- **Breathing Room**: Generous white space (premium feel)
- **Max Content Width**: 1280px (readable, elegant)
- **Grid System**: 12-column, mobile-first responsive
- **Padding**: Large vertical padding on sections (60-120px)

### Minimalist Principles
- Remove non-essential elements
- One focus per section
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Purposeful imagery only
- Generous whitespace
- Subtle, refined details

### Motion Design
- **Smooth Transitions**: 300-400ms ease-out
- **Subtle Scale**: Hover effects (0.02-0.03 scale)
- **Entrance Animations**: Fade + slide up (on scroll)
- **Loading States**: Smooth, not jarring
- **No Excessive Animation**: Less is more (premium feel)

## Conversion Optimization

### Call-to-Action Strategy

**Primary CTA**: "Schedule Free Consultation" / "Get Free Estimate"

**Tertiary CTA**: "View Gallery"

**CTA Placement:**
- Hero section (every page)
- After service overview

- Before/after gallery
- FAQ section
- Footer

### Form Strategy
- **Short forms** for quick contact (3-4 fields)
- **Detailed forms** for estimates (service-specific questions)
- **No mandatory fields** beyond name/phone/email
- **Clear next steps** after submission

### Social Proof Placement
- Testimonials on every service page
- Star ratings visible (Google, Yelp, etc.)
- Case studies with specific results
- Before/after galleries
- Social media feed integration

## Notes & Considerations

1. **Premium = Trustworthy**: In affluent markets, premium design + trust signals = higher conversion
2. **Local Matters**: Orange County focus is key differentiator vs. national chains
3. **Each Service is a Business**: Treat each service page as its own mini-business
4. **Process Reduces Anxiety**: Customers buy peace of mind - transparent process = confidence
5. **Motion is Subtle**: Premium brands move slowly, smoothly, with purpose
6. **White Space is Luxury**: Empty space feels expensive and premium
7. **Trust Badges Throughout**: Don't hide credentials - make them visible everywhere
